About Me

Hi I'm Hugh!

Tutoring Service

My goal is to help you succeed; sure, learn physics, but also how to get your desired grade!

After that, I am very flexible! Tutoring sessions should preferably be in-person (at a library, starbucks, or similar) since seeing how you solve problems is very important, but after initial sessions online sessions can be arranged. I believe students learn great from eachother, so depending on a students needs individual or small groups can work. After your first session, I will also include a repository of practice problems and solutions, as well as formula sheets as practice resources.
